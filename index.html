What We Stand For

The Four Pillars of NHS

Scholarship

A cumulative weighted GPA of 3.5 or higher, reflecting sustained academic excellence.

Service

Voluntary contribution to school and community, given freely and with a positive spirit.

Leadership

Resourceful, dependable students who contribute ideas and support their peers.

Character

Honesty, respect, and integrity demonstrated consistently in word and action.
