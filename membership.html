Membership

How to become a member, what's required to stay in good standing, and the key dates to know.

For Prospective Members

Eligibility & Application

Students with at least a 3.5 weighted GPA, based on their transcript at the end of second semester junior year, are initially eligible.

In mid-to-late February, all academically eligible juniors receive an emailed invitation to apply, sent to their NHCS email account. If you believe you meet the GPA requirement but haven't received an invitation by the end of February, contact your counselor right away, as the process moves quickly.

The invitation includes instructions for completing the application, which gives students the chance to showcase their service, leadership, character, and extracurricular involvement. Applicants have at least two weeks to complete and submit their application, and should contact an NHS adviser with any questions.

There is no fee to apply to NHS. If you receive an email requesting payment to apply, it is not from Laney NHS.

Applications must be submitted by the stated deadline; late submissions cannot be considered.

In September of senior year, a second opportunity to apply is offered to academically eligible students who (1) did not apply as juniors, (2) were not accepted as juniors, or (3) were not yet eligible as juniors but now hold a 3.5 weighted GPA. The process is the same, though it moves somewhat faster.

For Inducted Members

Ongoing Membership Requirements

Induction is not the finish line. Members are expected to continue reflecting the standards of scholarship, service, leadership, and character used as the basis for their selection.

Drop an induction ceremony photo

Every spring, our newest members are formally welcomed into the chapter at the Spring Induction Ceremony, a tradition held at Northside Baptist Church.

Maintain Scholarship

Members must maintain at least a 3.5 weighted GPA throughout their senior year, and continue to reflect the standards used as the basis for selection.

Attend Meetings

Held the first Thursday of each school month during Flex in the media center. Meetings are mandatory; members may not miss more than three. Anyone who misses a meeting must meet with an adviser for makeup.

Earn 30 Service Points

By May 6th, from a combination of individual projects, at least one group project offered monthly, mandatory meeting attendance, and at least 10 hours of school-related service, all pre-approved.

Pay Chapter Dues

$25 in dues is required, paid online through SchoolCashOnline by December 16th.

Meet the December Checkpoint

At least 12 service points must be earned by December 16th. Members who fall short are placed on probation and risk dismissal if unresolved by the January meeting.

Attend Spring Induction

All active members participate in the Spring Induction Ceremony each April at Northside Baptist Church.
